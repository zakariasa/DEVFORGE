Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

At Devforge, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it when you play our games published on the Amazon Appstore or visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect basic, anonymous usage data to help us improve our games. This includes:

  • Device information (e.g., model, OS version)
  • Game progress and crash reports
  • Non-personally identifiable gameplay statistics

2. How We Use the Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Improve game performance and fix bugs
  • Enhance user experience and gameplay features
  • Provide customer support

3. Third-Party Services

Our games may use third-party services (like analytics or advertising networks). These services have their own privacy policies addressing how they use your information.

4. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately to have it removed.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any modifications by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via email at support@devforgegame.com or through our Contact page.
